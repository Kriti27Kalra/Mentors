Engineering

GROUND ZERO

 

Aims & Objectives
›› Gradually enhances students' level from Foundation to Advanced.
›› Achieving high marks in Board Examinations.
›› Top Ranks in JEE (Main & Advanced), Science Olympiad & Mathematical Olympiad (optional).

Note :

1. Morning Batch Option : For the batch starting in April, after the completion of the first phase of class 11 course in July 2025, students can also opt for morning batch.
2. Based on their performance, students can join our Achiever/Star Batch in Class 11.

 

Eligibility : For class 11th Students . 

Duration :2 year Classroom Program

Course Description : Ground Zero is a course crafted for students moving from class 10 to class 11. This course is for students starting their preparation from scratch. This course will enhance the level of students from foundation to advanced, gradually preparing them for both Board examinations and JEE (Main & Advanced).

Class Scheduling :
›› 4 days a week (for batches starting from April/May)
›› 3-4 classes per day (any three/four of Physics, Chemistry, and Maths)
›› Additional English and Physical Education classes for Class 12 board examination.
›› Students may be called for additional classes during holidays and on Sundays at different timings.

Class Timing :
›› Class Timing: 08:00 AM - 01:50 PM/ 04:00 PM - 08:05 PM (2 options for each centre).

Course Completion :
››1st Year : January 2026
››2nd year : October/November 2026
››After Course Completion : Revision Classes and Test Series (September/October 2026 until JEE ADV. 2027)

 

For more details on the top batches, i.e., Achiever and Star for IIT-JEE/NEET, click on this link.

 

 

COUNT DOWN

Aims & Objectives
›› Preparing the students for both, Class 12 Board Examination and JEE (Main & Advanced).

Eligibility : For students in class 12th

Duration :1 year Classroom Program (Both Class 11 and Class 12 Syllabus covered).

Course Description : Countdown is a course designed to prepare students in class 12 for both Board examinations and JEE (Main & Advanced). The course also trains the students in techniques of stress handling and ensures that the students handle the examination pressure with ease.

Class Scheduling :
›› 4-5 days per week
›› 3-4 classes per day (any three/four of Physics, Chemistry, and Maths)
›› Additional English and Physical Education classes for class 12 board.

›› Students may be called for additional classes during the summer and winter holidays as well as on Sundays at different timings.

Class Timing :
›› 08:00 AM - 01:50 PM/ 04:00 PM - 08:05 PM (two options available at each centre).

Course Completion :

›› Class 12 syllabus will be completed by November 2025 and then important topics of Class 11 will be taught and completed by December 2025.
›› After Course Completion : Revision Classes and Test Series with discussion (September/October 2025 until JEE ADV. 2026)

 

REBOOST

Aims & Objectives
›› Specially designed for students aspiring for JEE Main and JEE Advanced.
›› Effectively focuses on JEE Main and JEE Advanced preparation.
›› Addressing gaps in their subject knowledge.
›› Equips the students with the skills and competencies required for their success in JEE Main and JEE Advanced.

Eligibility : For Class 12 passed Students

Duration : 1 year Classroom Program (Both class 11 and Class 12 syllabus covered).

Course Description : Reboost is a course specially designed for students going to join the battle once again. This course effectively focuses on JEE Main and JEE Advanced preparation by addressing gaps in their subject knowledge and equips students with the skills required for ther success.

Class Scheduling :
›› 5 to 6 days a Week (for Batches Starting from April/May/June)
›› 3-4 classes per day (any three/four of Physics, Chemistry, and Maths)

Class Timing :
›› For Boring Road/Kankarbagh/Bailey Road/Bazar Samiti - 8:00 AM-1:50 PM
›› Students may be called for additional classes during the summer and winter holidays as well as on Sundays at different timings.

Course Completion :
›› December 2025
››After Course Completion : Revision Classes and Test Series with Discussion (September/October 2025 until JEE Advanced 2026)
