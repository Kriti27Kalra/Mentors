Study Material

Study Material :

A well-researched and Exhaustive Study Material has been prepared by our esteemed team of Faculty members. Our Study Materials are pattern proof Courseware. Study Materials are also provided in digital format on ME-DIGITAL App.

(A) Special features of Study Material for ENGINEERING STREAM
Exhaustive Theory (Concept Boosters): The theory comprises of all the basic and fundamental concepts topic-wise followed by solved illustrations.
Daily Practice Problem (DPP): It is a set of questions incorporated within each topic in the study material. It provides a strong foundation to understand the concepts and must be solved and revised regularly.
Multiple Exercises: The Study Material has separate section of exercises for JEE MAIN, JEE ADVANCED, previous year's questions asked in JEE MAIN/ JEE ADVANCED & Subjective questions section.
Mentors Eduserv Rank Improvement Package [MERIP] : MERIP for JEE MAIN (Containing 2400+ challenging problems on JEE MAIN Pattern), MERIP for JEE ADVANCED (Containing 1800+ challenging problems on JEE ADVANCED Pattern).

(B) Special features of Study Material for MEDICAL STREAM
Exhaustive Theory (Concept Boosters): The theory comprises of all the basic and fundamental concepts topic-wise followed by solved illustrations.
Daily Practice Problem (DPP): It is a set of questions incorporated within each topic in the study material. It provides a strong foundation to understand the concepts and must be solved and revised regularly.
Multiple Exercises: The Study Material has separate section of exercises for NEET along with previous year's questions asked in NEET.
Mentors Eduserv Rank Improvement Package [MERIP] : MERIP for NEET (Containing 3500+ challenging problems on NEET (Pattern).
(C) Special features of regular Study Materials for FOUNDATION STREAM (Class 6, 7, 8, 9 & 10) students are as follows:
Students are provided regular study materials for Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry & Biology. In addition, students are also provided classes & study materials for Mental Ability & Social Studies as per the National Talent Search Examination (NTSE*) Syllabus.

  • Exhaustive Theory (Concept Boosters): The theory comprises of all the basic and fundamental concepts topic-wise followed by solved illustrations.
  • Some higher level concepts (other than NCERT syllabus) are also incorporated.
  • Daily Practice Problem (DPP): It is a set of questions incorporated within each topic in the study material. It provides a strong foundation to understand the concepts and must be solved and revised regularly.
  • NCERT Exercise.Exercises based on future competitive examinations containing questions based on JEE (MAIN & ADVANCED) / NEET pattern. These exercises contains single correct type multiple choice questions, multiple correct type multiple choice questions, passage type questions, column-matching type questions, assertion-reason type questions & integer type questions.
  • Exclusive Booklet for NTSE: A separate NTSE Booklet is provided to the students containing summarized theory and exercises. There are 2 types of exercises, one containing questions based on NTSE pattern while the other containing previous year's NTSE questions. Students are provided the NTSE booklets for Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Mental Ability & S.ST.
  • Exclusive Booklets for Junior Science OLYMPIAD & Mathematics OLYMPIAD (conducted by HBCSE): A separate OLYMPIAD Booklet are provided to the students containing summarized theory and exercises. There are 2 types of exercises, one containing questions based on Junior Science OLYMPIAD and Mathematics OLYMPIAD pattern while the other containing previous year's Junior Science OLYMPIAD and Mathematics OLYMPIAD questions.

Note : Special subject - wise assignments are also provided after course completion for School/Board exam preparation.
